Subteams:

Subteams of Team 342
Specification Team name Description
Computational/Digital Media The media team deals with social media for the team's Facebook, Twitter, etc. as well as photos, and videos the team makes and uses.
Programming The programming puts code on the robot and makes it move with use of a controller as well as autonomous functions for the robot.
Physical Electrical The electrical team deals with calculating how much power a specific element of the robot needs as well as wiring of elements.
Mechanical The mechanical team deals with aspects such as the physical design, implementation, and building of the robot.
Safety Safety Team The safety subteam ensures that everyone is keeping safe within the team as well as ensuring that documents such as Material Safety Data Sheets are kept up to date and well organized.
Competition Drive Team The drive team is the subteam focused on driving the physical robot and practices driving and operating functions of the robot for competition.

Mentors:

Team 342 has many mentors including those of alumni status.
