FIRST Robotics Team 342 - The Burning Magnetos

Quick Summary:

We are Team 342 the Burning Magnetos.
We are part of the organization called FIRST which you can read more about here.
Team 342 is a part of the FIRST Robotics Competition where teams from all around the world compete in a predesigned game with a robot built by high school students.
Teams must build a robot within about 6 weeks from scratch with help from mentors and alumni with many different backgrounds.
Once built robots must be programmed for both autonomous and teleoperated control to compete on the field.
Our team has multiple subteams which you can read more about on the about page.
