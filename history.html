History


We are Team 342 - The Burning Magnetos.


Team 342 was founded in 1999 and attended its first competition in the year 2000.

Since then we've been partnered with BOSCH and to pay homage to them we've decided to name ourselves after one of their most important machine parts. The Magneto.

Since then many mentors, students, and alumni have come and gone; but, not without gaining unforgettable experiences and knowledge.

We thank you for taking the time to learn about our history and while this isn't a comprehensive list of everything that makes our team, this still covers the major stepping stones on our journey.
